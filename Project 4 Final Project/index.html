"Foxes are small to medium-sized, omnivorous mammals belonging to several genera of the family Canidae. They have a flattened skull, upright triangular ears, a pointed, slightly upturned snout, and a long bushy tail (or brush)." - Wikipedia

"Foxes live on every continent except Antarctica. The most common and widespread species of fox is the red fox (Vulpes vulpes) with about 47 recognized subspecies" - Wikipedia

On this page, you can enter a name to generate a pet fox! Simply enter a name and click generate. Clicking the button also generates an image of your new fox (if you're unsure which fox is yours due to there being multiple in the picture, just pick whichever one you like the most). You can also learn more about your fox with the generated characteristics!
