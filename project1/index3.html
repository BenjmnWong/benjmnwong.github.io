Here are my feelings towards the game:

  • I think this game is fun in small doses because it's so rage-inducing even for someone who typically doesn't get mad at video games.
  • It's really team dependent and if you are matched with stupid people or people who are trying to lose it's really frustrating. This is why I try to play with 3-5 people I know so I can actually enjoy myself.
  • The only reason I actually play is that a few of my friends do and it's really fun to play with them and only them. If I didn't have any friends who played the game it would be a complete waste of time but I do genuinely enjoy getting better and winning with my friends.
